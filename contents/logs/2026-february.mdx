title: "Against the Noise"
---

February was intense, as only months influenced by outside noise can be. World events such as international scandals, wars, and crises were unfolding at the same time. It's difficult to handle the weight of the world when it hits you all at once. I let it upset me more than I would have liked. But the response was familiar: let the pain sink in, close the tabs, and leave the social feeds. I sit at my desk with a notebook, a pen, and a good book, letting my mind wander. During these confusing times, the most productive thing I can do is focus on the basics. I just work hard and be responsible only for my work and the people who matter. Sometimes, that is enough. You just have to be the best version of yourself for the people who care about you.

I was surprised by my reaction. After months of work, I finished creating my personal website and wrote my first article in years for anyone to read. I spent most of that time thinking about what matters to me, my goals, and my ideas. This helped me create a clearer plan for how and what to work on and what I should do to improve. On a personal level, I kept up with my training, managed my time wisely, and planned a short trip to the [Langhe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Langhe) for next month. These small things have helped. Although I felt discouraged and useless at times, I took action, and that's what matters most. We are all humans in the end.
